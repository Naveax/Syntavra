# Community Conduct

SignalCore contributors and users must keep technical collaboration professional, specific and safe.

Expected conduct:

- discuss code, evidence and reproducible behavior rather than attacking people;
- respect privacy, licenses, attribution and security boundaries;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing and deliberately disruptive behavior;
- disclose conflicts of interest and benchmark limitations;
- report security-sensitive material privately.

Maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions and participation that violate these rules. Serious or repeated violations may result in repository restrictions.
